# 📊 Team Performance, Revenue & Marketing ROI Dashboard

**Organization:** Kings Builders — Head Office  
**Tenure:** 2021 – 2025  
**Tool:** Microsoft Power BI  
**Author:** Mubinah Baiyg

---

## 📌 Overview

A multi-page Power BI dashboard built to track and analyze business performance 
across sales teams, revenue growth, and marketing ROI for Kings Builders' 
real estate projects over a 5-year period (2021–2025).

---

## 📂 Dashboard Pages

### 1. 🏠 Intro
Title page with project overview and authorship information.

### 2. 👥 Team Performance Analysis
Tracks individual and team-level sales performance across 48 agents.

**Key KPIs:**
- Total Revenue: **10bn**
- Total Deals: **766**
- Average Deal Size: **13M**
- Monthly Revenue Rate: **165M**
- Revenue Per Agent: **215M**

**Visuals include:**
- Performance vs Tenure scatter chart
- Deal Distribution across team members
- Monthly Performance Comparison (stacked bar)
- Top 5 Revenue Performers (donut chart)
- Agent Revenue Growth Since Start
- Sales Performance Over Years (2021–2025)

### 4. 📣 Marketing Campaign Performance & ROI Analysis
Evaluates campaign effectiveness across 5 social media pages.

**Key KPIs:**
- Total Revenue: **10bn**
- Total Marketing Spend: **7M**
- ROAS: **1.48K**
- ROAS Percentage: **147.78K**
- Net Profit: **10bn**
- Profitable Pages: **5**

**Visuals include:**
- Bubble chart: Marketing Spend vs Revenue by Page
- Top Performing Campaigns by ROAS (bar chart)
- ROAS Trend 2023–2025 Decline Analysis
- Contribution to Total Profit (waterfall chart)

**Key Insights highlighted:**
- Zavia delivers highest ROAS → scale this campaign
- Kings Real Estate has high spend but lower efficiency
- Overall ROAS declining YoY → campaign fatigue or poor targeting
